02 октября (среда)

Докладчик: Эльмира Калимулина (с.н.с., МГУ)
Тема: “Some Aspects of Research on Queuing Networks with Dynamic Structure”
Время: 18:30
Аудитория: 16-13
Аннотация: This work examines specific models of queuing networks where the network structure evolves over time. The ergodicity of Jackson networks on random graphs is studied, along with the behaviour of these networks depending on the dynamics of their structure. Some well-known approaches are applied, and it is shown that similar results to classical ones can be obtained even under relaxed conditions.
